In this game, the scoring technique depends on a confident factor.
Before answering the question, the players should reply whether they are confident with their answer or that they are not so sure and need help.
If the player is confident and chose the correct answer, then he/she will gain 2 points. But if the answer was wrong he/she will be fined 2 points.
meanwhile, if the player was not so sure and asked for help. He/she will be directed to a page with a hint option.
if the answer then was correct, the player will gain 1 point, and if wrong, no deduction will happen.
This method encourages the player to seek help and search more before answering.
